## Palette

- Deep teal: `#173F46`
- Teal shadow: `#0E343B`
- Warm ivory: `#F7F2EA`
- Pale stone: `#E5DACC`
- Muted brass: `#B8905B`
- Sage wash: `#D8E5E1`
- Warm gray: `#7B7065`

Use brass only as a small premium accent. Preserve strong contrast for text and controls.
