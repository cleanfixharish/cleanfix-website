# CleanFixHarish Visual System V2

Status: working visual direction for owner review.

## Brand character

Calm local competence expressed with the dimensional craft of the approved Home Support Emblem. The interface should feel like a carefully made application, not a decorative luxury advertisement.

## Core palette

- Deep navy-teal: `#102E38`
- Navy highlight: `#163F49`
- Ink shadow: `#081F28`
- Warm ivory: `#F7F2EA`
- Soft champagne: `#E8D8BE`
- Rich antique gold: `#C49332`
- Gold highlight: `#F0C96F`
- Bronze shadow: `#6F4818`
- Accessible body text on ivory: `#24373C`

Gold is reserved for symbols, borders, small labels, focus details, and premium highlights. Body text and essential controls must keep strong flat contrast.

## Typography direction

- English display: Cormorant Garamond, with restrained weight and generous optical spacing.
- Hebrew display: Noto Serif Hebrew.
- English interface/body: Manrope.
- Hebrew interface/body: Noto Sans Hebrew.
- Uppercase labels use wide tracking sparingly; paragraphs never use metallic effects.

Final fonts should be self-hosted for performance and privacy before production.

## Golden-ratio system

- Primary ratio: `1 : 1.618`.
- Hero split: `61.8% / 38.2%`.
- Spacing scale: `8, 13, 21, 34, 55, 89px`.
- Image cards: `1.618 : 1` landscape or `1 : 1` service tiles.
- Main focal points sit near `38.2%` or `61.8%`, never mechanically centered everywhere.
- Border radius scale: `13, 21, 34px`.

## Surface and symbol treatment

- Navy panels use a quiet tonal gradient and soft inner depth.
- Gold symbols use a champagne-to-bronze gradient, a fine highlight, and a restrained shadow.
- Ivory surfaces use subtle warmth and generous negative space.
- No fake chrome, glitter, excessive glow, or gold body copy.

## Photography

- Realistic local apartments and believable service work.
- Deep navy-teal workwear creates continuity with the logo.
- Warm ivory, pale oak, limestone, and small brass details form the environment.
- Natural Mediterranean daylight; never a heavy gold filter.
- Honest expressions, correct tools, safe work, and no visible third-party logos.

## Accessibility and performance

- All essential information remains text, not embedded in images.
- Decorative embossed effects cannot reduce contrast.
- Motion respects reduced-motion preferences.
- Gold symbols use one restrained pointer-hover light sweep; it is disabled on touch-only devices and when reduced motion is requested.
- Responsive JPEGs are generated from protected high-resolution PNG review masters.
