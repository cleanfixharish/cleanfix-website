## Constraints

### Visual System (CleanFixHarish Brand)
- **Personality**: Dependable, premium, calm, precise, local
- **Primary Color**: Soft Sky Blue (HSL 205 65% 55%)
- **Accent Color**: Fresh Sage Green (HSL 150 35% 48%)
- **Neutrals**: Warm Beige, Light Stone Gray, Soft Silver
- **Body Font**: Inter (clean, modern, geometric)
- **Heading Font**: Lora (serif, premium feel)
- **Photography**: Photorealistic, bright natural daylight, soft shadows, clean white interiors
- **Icons**: Minimal flat design, rounded corners, consistent line thickness
- **Layout**: Golden Ratio (38% text / 62% visual in hero sections)
- **Tone**: Quiet confidence, cleanliness, professionalism — no loud marketing
